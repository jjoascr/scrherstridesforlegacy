Why We’re Partnering

Black Girl Ventures Foundation expands access to community, capital, and capacity-building for under-resourced founders. Our Women’s History Month effort contributes to the BGV NextGen Program—supporting emerging leaders and future innovators.

Your participation directly supports leadership development and access to capital for the next generation oflegacy builders.

Meet Our Committee

SCR Women's History Month

As a committee, we are deeply excited to lead this important work — honoring the women who paved the way, celebrating those shaping impact today, and inspiring those who will lead tomorrow.

Women’s History Month is not just a moment of reflection. It is an opportunity to educate, elevate, and engage our region in meaningful ways. Through thoughtful initiatives, educational programming, and legacy-centered storytelling, we seek to highlight the strides of women across time.

We look forward to bringing forward initiatives that inform, empower, and create space for women’s excellence to be both seen and celebrated.
